The audience

Who you're reaching

her girls club brings together women in Basel who are curious, engaged and culturally aware — and who act on recommendations from people and communities they trust.

Our community spans beauty, wellness, fashion and lifestyle. They attend our events in person, follow us on Instagram and share their experiences authentically. When a brand shows up at a her girls club event, it shows up in context — as a natural, trusted part of their world.

We work exclusively with brands whose values align with our community — which means every integration feels genuine, not forced.

How we work together

Partnership formats

Four ways your brand can show up authentically within the her girls club community.

01
Brand Activations & Events

We design and co-host curated experiences that bring your brand to life — from intimate launch evenings to immersive pop-up experiences crafted to leave a lasting impression with our community.

02
Product Placements

Ensure your products are seamlessly integrated into the her girls club experience — goodie bags, gifting, tastings and on-site presence seen and shared by a curated circle of community members.

03
Community Integration

Authentic storytelling through bespoke campaigns connecting your brand with our community through exclusive events, content and experiences that feel natural — because they live inside a community that already trusts us.

04
Community Campaigns

Longer-term partnerships that embed your brand as a meaningful part of the her girls club world — across multiple events, social touchpoints and community moments throughout the year.
